How it works

Broken doesn't mean done.

Fixify has a team of experienced fixers who will fix your broken electronics, small appliances, clothing, bikes, jewelry, computers, toys, and more. Every repair starts with a bid from your fixer, and it will never cost more than replacing the item.

Step 1

Tell us what's broken

Fill out a quick fix request: name the item, add a photo, and describe what's wrong. It takes about two minutes.

Step 2

A fixer takes it on

One of our fixers claims your request, sends you a bid and timeline, and arranges drop-off with you in the built-in chat.

Step 3

Pick it up fixed

You'll get a message the moment it's ready. Swing by, grab your item, and get back to using it.

FAQ

Good questions, quick answers.

What does it cost?+
Your fixer gives you a bid before any work starts, and it will never be more than the price of buying a replacement. On average we aim for about 40% of the cost of a new one, built on a $5/hour base rate for our fixers. Donations are always welcome too.
What can I bring?+
Electronics, small appliances, clothing and textiles, bikes, jewelry, computers, toys, and more. Not sure if we'd take it? Submit a request anyway. Worst case, we'll point you in the right direction.
How do drop-off and pickup work?+
Once a fixer claims your request, they'll message you in the built-in chat to arrange drop-off. When the repair is done, you'll get another message that it's ready to pick up.
How long does a repair take?+
Your fixer gives you a bid and an estimated timeline when they claim your item. Simple fixes take a few days, trickier ones a couple of weeks. You can check in over chat anytime.
What if it can't be fixed?+
Sometimes a thing really is beyond saving. If so, we'll tell you what we found and suggest a plan B: spare parts, recycling, or advice on a replacement. You only pay if we fix it. Either way, you'll know.
